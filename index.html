About Quick Fix

Your trusted local electronics and appliance repair specialists

Professional Repair Services Since 2023

Quick Fix has been serving the Melton Rose and surrounding communities with reliable, affordable, and high-quality electronics and appliance repair services. Our mission is to extend the life of your valuable appliances while saving you money compared to buying new replacements.

We take pride in our team of certified technicians who undergo regular training to stay updated with the latest technology and repair techniques. Our commitment to quality repairs, honest service, and customer satisfaction has made us the go-to repair centre in the area.

Located conveniently at 4 Melton Rd, Shop 1, we offer both in-shop and on-site repair services for your convenience. We understand how important your appliances are to your daily life, which is why we prioritize quick turnaround times without compromising on quality.
